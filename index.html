My Family

My family isn't that large, since I am an only child, and therefore don't have any siblings. My father is a buisness man who works every weekday to help elderly people enjoy their day at his facility, called Agape. My mother is a pharmacist who works in the same building that my father owns. She provides the medication that the elders need for their wellbeing. I am also the owner of 2 cats, with the names of Atom and Crystal. They are both black and white, two littermates amongst a litter of 4 kittens. My parents have provided me the tools to success, and I can't thank them enough for it. They put me on the road to become better every day, and they have helped me more times than I can count. They have taught me many necessary attributes in life, which will help me throughout my years. That is why my family is so important to me.

Hobbies

I have mutiple hobbies, including reading and playing with my cats. I love to read, mostly fiction and fantasy, though sometimes I will pick up a nonfiction book. Some of my favorite series include Harry Potter, Percy Jackson, Heroes of Olympus, Wings of Fire, Warriors, the Seven Realms, and the Kingdom Keepers. I could read all day if I wanted to. I usually read with my kindle, since it damages my eyes less, but I also read using a real book as well. Reading helps me to learn new words in a fun way, and imagine stories of my own. I believe that reading is essential in becoming a better writer and reader, which is why I think that reading is a great hobby to have! I hope that you love to read books as well. I encourage all of the people out there to read at home, especially during the pandemic, when people are suffering from depression. Reading will fill the gap of talking to friends in person. Also, playing with my cats helps me to relax and enjoy the day. My cats are like a stress reliever, and whenever I feel bad, my cats always help me feel better. It helps both them and myself to have some fun during the day. Those are the reasons why I love having these hobbies!
